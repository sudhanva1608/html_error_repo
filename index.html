Stress In India: Quick Stats

1 in 7 People

In 2017, an estimated 197.3 million people in India were living with mental disorders.

Source: Lancet Psychiatry (GBD 1990-2017), published February 2020

10.6% Current Morbidity

India's National Mental Health Survey (2015-16) estimated 10.6% current mental morbidity and 13.7% lifetime morbidity.

Source: NMHS 2015-16 (MoHFW/NIMHANS)

1,70,924 Suicides (2022)

NCRB recorded 1,70,924 suicides in 2022, with an all-India suicide rate of 12.4 per lakh.

Source: NCRB ADSI 2022, Chapter 2

Student Stress Signal

Students accounted for 7.6% of suicide deaths in 2022, i.e., 13,044 student suicides.

Source: NCRB ADSI 2022, profession-wise distribution

Help-Seeking Is Rising

Tele-MANAS handled 34.34 lakh+ calls since launch (October 10, 2022) up to March 3, 2026.

Source: PIB MoHFW update dated March 23, 2026

Top Recorded Causes

In 2022, the two largest recorded causes of suicide were family problems (31.7%) and illness (18.4%).

Source: NCRB ADSI 2022 cause-wise analysis
